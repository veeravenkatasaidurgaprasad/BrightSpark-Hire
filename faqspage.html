Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply for a job? +
You can apply directly through the job listings page by clicking the "Apply" button and filling in your details.
What training programs are offered? +
We offer 2-week, 1-month, and 3-month hospitality training programs, all with job guarantee options.
How do I get my certificate after completing a training? +
Once you successfully complete a training program, your certificate will be available in the Certificates page under your profile.
Can I request a password reset? +
Yes, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page to receive a password reset link via your registered email.
