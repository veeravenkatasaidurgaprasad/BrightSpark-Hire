Privacy Policy

At Hospitality Jobs, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, and phone number.
  • Job applications and training program enrollments.
  • Device and usage information for improving our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect job seekers with employers.
  • To process training program enrollments and certifications.
  • To provide customer support and resolve issues.
  • To send important updates, offers, and notifications.

3. Data Sharing

We may share your information only with trusted employers, trainers, and service providers strictly for recruitment and training purposes. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your personal data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee complete protection against cyber risks.

5. Cookies

Our site uses cookies to improve user experience, remember preferences, and analyze site performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Request a copy of your stored data (as per GDPR).

7. International Users

Since we operate in both the UK and India, your data may be stored or processed across these regions in compliance with applicable laws.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our services constitutes agreement with the updated version.
