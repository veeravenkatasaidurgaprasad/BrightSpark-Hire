About Us

At BrightSpark Hire Consultancy Solutions, we believe in creating real opportunities for individuals aspiring to build a career in the hospitality industry. Based in the UK, we specialize in connecting job seekers with leading hotels, agencies, and employers across London and beyond.

We go beyond just job placement—we prepare you for success. With our hospitality training programs, including industry-standard tools such as Opera PMS, Cloud and other essential systems, you’ll gain the skills and confidence needed to excel in your role. Our training comes with certifications recognized by employers, giving you a competitive edge.

What sets us apart is our Job Guarantee Program. We ensure that every candidate who joins us secures employment within 90 days of application. From the moment you begin your journey with us, our dedicated team supports you every step of the way—training, mentoring, interview preparation, and direct connections to employers.

Whether you are seeking part-time or full-time roles, we are committed to guiding you until you land your dream job.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What kind of jobs do you offer?
We provide part-time and full-time opportunities in the hospitality sector, including roles in hotels, restaurants, and agencies across London.
2. Do you really guarantee a job within 90 days?
Yes! We have partnerships with leading hotels and recruitment agencies in London. Once you complete your training and certification, we ensure you are placed in a suitable role within 90 days of your application.
3. What is Opera Cloud?
Opera Cloud (Property Management System) is a global hotel software used by major hotels for reservations, check-ins, billing, and customer management. Training on Opera PMS gives you an advantage and makes you job-ready from day one.
4. Who can apply?
Our program is open to:

• UK residents seeking part-time or full-time hospitality jobs
• International students and newcomers who want to start a career
• Anyone looking to gain practical training and certifications to improve their job prospects
5. Do I need prior experience in hospitality?
No prior experience is required. We provide complete training and support to help beginners as well as those looking to upgrade their skills.
6. What certifications will I receive?
You will receive industry-recognized certifications in hospitality operations and systems (including Opera PMS), which are valued by employers in the UK.
7. How long does the training take?
Our training is short and intensive, designed to prepare you quickly for the job market. The duration depends on your chosen program, but most candidates complete it within a few weeks.
8. Will you support me after training?
Yes! Our team provides continuous support until you secure employment. We help with CV preparation, interview training, and connecting you with the right employers.
9. Are there any fees for training and job placement?
We provide training, certification, and guaranteed job placement at affordable program fees. (You can add details here about pricing and our team will contact you.)
10. How do I get started?
It’s simple:

1. Apply online through our website.
2. Join our training and certification program.
3. Get placed in a guaranteed job within 90 days!
